Computer Science Grad to SCADA Programmer

After graduating with a Computer Science degree, I discovered the world of Industrial Automation — a niche that blends software engineering with real-world operational technology. SCADA development allowed me to leverage my programming background to build real-time systems for monitoring, control, and data-driven decision-making in industries like water transfer, oil & gas, and manufacturing.

The field offers an incredible opportunity for software-minded individuals willing to bridge IT and OT, particularly with the rise of IIoT, MQTT Unified Namespace structures, and Industry 4.0 standards.
