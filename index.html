About Me

I'm a SCADA Developer with a Computer Science background, specializing in building industrial IIoT systems that connect field operations to enterprise-level insights. I focus on scalable architectures with MQTT Unified Namespace, Ignition SCADA, custom Python-based telemetry solutions, and deploying resilient networking with Peplink routers and remote cellular solutions.
